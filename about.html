This web app is intended to help you keep "in tune".
It works by detecting and displaying your voice pitch - you should try to keep your voice at the pitch you intended. To help you, you can sing along to a scale.

You have two on/off buttons on the left:
start/stop voice - shows the pitch (note) of your voice
start/stop scale - shows the note as a scale is played.

You can set your Vocal range in the next box on the right. The vocal range will also define the scale that you can sing along to.
For example, if you set the "contralto" range, which covers the 2 octaves from F3 to F5 (by the way, middle C is called C4) then the scale will be F major.

You can change the scale in the next box, using some sliders:
The top slider changes the tempo (beats per minute).
The second slider changes the note length (between a quarter note and a full note).
The last slider changes the number of octaves of the scale - either the lower, or the upper, or both octaves.
